A focused faculty pilot is enough

The goal is not to restructure all teaching immediately. The goal is to show that one professor-side workflow can become more structured, reusable, and easier to review.
Suggested pilot structure

Pilot scope

  • One course or module
  • One corpus of existing teaching sources
  • One review cycle with the professor
  • One structured lesson package output

Expected outcomes

  • Reduced manual preparation effort
  • Cleaner lesson structure
  • Improved delegation to assistants
  • Better continuity across future semesters
Recommended framing: one faculty member, one course, one controlled source set, and one clearly reviewable teaching output.

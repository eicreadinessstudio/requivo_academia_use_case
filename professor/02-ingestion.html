Step 1 · Ingestion

Connect existing teaching material

Requivo ingests the professor’s existing teaching sources without requiring them to be manually rewritten or pre-cleaned first.

Slide decks, private notes, paper packs, course outlines, and case material all become one structured teaching evidence space that can support lesson preparation.

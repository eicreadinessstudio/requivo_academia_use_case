If critical process logic remains dependent on a few senior staff:
Onboarding slows — new administrative staff cannot act independently without personal clarification
Inconsistency grows — offices and teams begin operating from slightly different versions of the same process
Escalation becomes routine — small ambiguities repeatedly return to senior staff instead of being resolved once at system level
Institutional continuity stays fragile — a single absence, handover, or role change can break a critical workflow

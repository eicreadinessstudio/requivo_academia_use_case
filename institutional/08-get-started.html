Requivo supports early pilot conversations with research-intensive academic institutions. A narrow, high-signal pilot is the right starting point.

A narrow pilot is a stronger pilot

The goal is not enterprise-wide rollout on day one. The goal is to demonstrate that fragmented process evidence can become one governed institutional layer with measurable value.
Suggested pilot structure

Pilot scope

  • One process family: externally funded project initiation
  • One controlled evidence corpus
  • Two to three institutional validators
  • One governed process layer as output

Expected outcomes

  • Reduced reconstruction effort
  • Clearer process ownership
  • Earlier detection of ambiguity
  • Reusable governed knowledge for future workflows
Recommended framing: one university, one administrative process family, one controlled corpus, and a clearly reviewable governed output.

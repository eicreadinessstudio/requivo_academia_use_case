Step 1 · Ingestion

Connect what the university already has

Requivo ingests the institution’s existing academic and administrative evidence: SOPs, grant guidance, archived proposals, wiki fragments, and email-derived process clarifications.

The pilot does not start from a clean system. It starts from the reality of fragmented university process material already distributed across offices, folders, templates, and decisions.

2 / 8

Evidence before governance

Requivo begins by turning scattered institutional material into one analysable evidence space. The purpose is not to assume the university is already structured, but to make fragmented process knowledge legible enough to work with.
Source connectors
824 evidence objects normalised from 7 academic sources. Each is typed, dated, linked to origin, and ready for knowledge construction.

Administrative use case corpus

Messy by design

The value of the pilot is not in a curated toy dataset, but in dealing with the actual evidence mess universities already operate with.

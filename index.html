Pilot partner walkthrough · University / research environment

From fragmented academic procedures to governed research operations.

This demo shows how Requivo can turn dispersed grant, ethics, SOP, onboarding, and project-execution knowledge into a governed operational knowledge layer for an academic institution.

Institution Helix University Faculty of Translational Life Sciences
Target pain Research operations Grant preparation, ethics, SOPs, onboarding
Pilot goal Governed clarity One verified procedure layer across teams
Why this partner cares
Knowledge is split across many repositories Grant templates, ethics documents, SharePoint folders, old proposals, SOP PDFs, email decisions, and internal wiki fragments.
Critical process knowledge is expert-dependent Research managers and study coordinators know the real sequence, but much of it is not captured in one governed place.
New staff need faster onboarding Postdocs, project managers, and PhD candidates lose time trying to reconstruct submission and study-startup logic.

Demo flow

Suggested pilot scope 1 faculty or institute · 1 operational process family · 1 controlled corpus · expert validation loop with 2–3 academic stakeholders.

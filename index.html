How could Requivo support a medical university?

Explore two pilot-relevant use cases for a Sicilian medical university: one institutional workflow and one professor-side teaching workflow.

What this demo shows

Requivo turns fragmented academic evidence into governed operational knowledge. This walkthrough shows how that logic can support both university operations and faculty teaching preparation.

Suggested pilot framing

Start with one narrow institutional workflow and one faculty-side teaching use case. Keep the corpus controlled, the validators clear, and the output highly reviewable.

How could Requivo support a medical university?

Explore two pilot-relevant use cases for a Sicilian medical university: one institutional workflow and one professor-side teaching workflow.

Requivo turns fragmented academic evidence into governed operational knowledge. This demo is designed to show how that logic could support both university operations and faculty teaching preparation.
